Common questions

Can I really get a good website for $499?

Yes – as long as the scope is clear and focused. The Smart Starter package is designed for lean, effective sites that avoid unnecessary complexity. If you need advanced features, SimplyTech will quote those separately so you can decide.

What extra costs should I expect?

You’ll pay ongoing hosting and domain fees (usually between $10–$30 per month depending on platform), plus any subscriptions such as Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace if you choose cloud email. These are billed directly to you by the provider.

Do I own my website and domain?

Yes. All accounts and licences are created in your name. SimplyTech documents logins and settings so you retain full control even if we stop working together one day.

I’m not technical. How much do I have to manage?

If you enjoy maintaining things yourself, you’ll get training and notes. If you’d rather not touch anything, a Care plan means SimplyTech handles all updates and technical bits for a modest monthly fee.
